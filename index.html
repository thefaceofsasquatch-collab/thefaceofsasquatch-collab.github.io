The Window. The Fingernails. The Eyes.

SASQUATCH THEORY · Ohio · April 2026

A witness in Ohio reports hearing something outside their window late at night. When they looked, a massive face was peering in — hands resting on the window frame, cracked black fingernails visible against the glass. The eyes were described as solid black, unblinking, and intelligent. The subject did not flee when the witness moved. It watched. Then it was gone.

This account was the most-sighted video in the April dataset with the highest engagement score. The window-peering behavior appears as a recurring pattern across 4 separate reports this month.
